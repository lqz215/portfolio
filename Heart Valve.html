Project: Heart Valve

An Engineering Project

The goal of this project is to design a new type of one way non-return heart valve project. Our team had a meeting with our clients, who are the members of the bio-medical team, to discuss the purpose of the heart valve that has already on the market and problems they have. The two important constraints of the heart valve is preventing the blood from flowing backwards and maximizing the quantity of blood flow. We designed a heart valve that has a lift-slide blocker in it. When the blood comes in, the blocker would slide down until it's blocked by the little pump that is at the edge of the valve, the blood would flow through the gap between the blocker and the valve. When the blood flow backwards, the blood would push the blocker up and block the whole heart valve. This is a very simple and economy design which has only two parts and could help patients' hearts to work properly.

I also knew how to express the idea clearly and make a sketch of the product that showed its function. This project was a big step for me.
