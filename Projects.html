Heart Valve

Course: Introduction to Engineering Design

Date: 3/9/18-3/30/18

Objective: To design a one way non-return heart valve for future patient

The goal of this project is to design a new type of one way non-return heart valve project. Our team had a meeting with our clients, who are the members of the bio-medical team, to discuss the purpose of the heart valve that has already on the market and problems they have. The two important constraints of the heart valve is preventing the blood from flowing backwards and maximizing the quantity of blood flow. n this project, I learned how to make advanced modeling in the inventor. Now I've understood the association between the toughness and the thickness, which let me know how should I balance these two variable. The most valuable skill I've gained in this project is that I've learnt how to communicate with other team member and how to lead the team effectively. As the team leader, I kept tracking our clients' demands and introducing our new idea of design to them. The whole project was going very well in a highly effective way. I also knew how to express the idea clearly and make a sketch of the product that showed its function. This project was a big step for me.

Text Adventure Project

Course: Computer Science Principle

Date: 5/11/18-5/21/18

Objective: To create a story which the readers get to have their choices that can affect the storyline.

During this text adventure project, we used some interesting decision variables to keep people stay in track, We also try to shorten the plotline to avoid useless variables. We keep in mind that we need to make sure that the player can decide the end of the story. During the coding we put some easter eggs in the code. For example, if you enter something other than a 1, 2, or e in the first decision it the system will say that you fell off from the chair. I heard some game company also put some easter egg in their game to boost their game culture. Also, during the coding process, we try to make the code to be user-proof, which means debug as many errors as we can to prevent them from breaking our code. In this project, I started to know the logical principle of the python function. Whatsmore, in this project, I made lots of effort to organize my indentation and function format. This project is really a important step for me in the engineering.
